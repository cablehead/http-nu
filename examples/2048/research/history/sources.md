# Sources

Annotated index of every URL cited across the chapters, grouped by
primary vs. secondary. Primary = written by people directly involved.
Secondary = press / encyclopedia / analysis.

## Primary

- **Gabriele Cirulli, "2048, success and me"** -- Cirulli's own
  retrospective Medium post, written a few weeks after launch. The
  cleanest first-hand account of his motivations, his refusal to
  monetise initially, and his acknowledgement of *Threes!*.
  <https://medium.com/@gabrielecirulli/2048-success-and-me-7dc664f7a9bd>

- **Asher Vollmer and Greg Wohlwend, "Threemails"** -- the 45,000-word
  Threes! development retrospective, assembled from 570+ emails.
  Contains the "broken game" critique of 2048 and the "imitation
  happens after we've had time to descend slowly from the peak"
  argument.
  <https://asherv.com/threes/threemails/>

- **Hacker News, "2048"** -- the original launch post by Cirulli,
  9 March 2014. About 3,000 upvotes; Cirulli replies in-thread.
  <https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=7373566>

- **Hacker News, "2048, success and me"** -- the discussion thread
  that accompanied Cirulli's Medium post.
  <https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=7704800>
