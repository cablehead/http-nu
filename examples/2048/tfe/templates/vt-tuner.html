three sequential phases on one view-transition:
  1. slide (+ ghost fade)
  2. merge pop (delay = slide)
  3. spawn (delay = slide + merge)
slide duration how long every surviving tile takes to move (ms)
ghost duration how long the consumed merge tile takes to fade (ms)
merge duration how long the survivor's scale-pop takes (ms)
merge scale peak scale at the top of the pop (1 = no pop)
spawn duration how long the new tile takes to fade + scale in (ms)
spawn from-scale how small the new tile starts (1 = no scale)
